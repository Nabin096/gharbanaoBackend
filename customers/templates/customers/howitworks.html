How It Works?

Get Started

Tell us about the space, the way you want it to look, the amount you are willing to spend etc. The list of designers to choose from will be generated based on these data.

Choose designer

From the list generated, choose a designer you would like to work with. While browsing through the list, you can open each designer's profile and check their portfolio.

Create a Project

Provide your email id and phone no. to create your new project. A ProjectID and password will be mailed to you. Login using this data to view your Project Dashboard.

Schedule Call

Pick a time, in the next 48 hours, when we can reach you via phone call. You can explain your project in detail and mention additional requirements, if any, during the call.

Start Working

Once your project is recieved and accepted by the designer, his/her contact details will be shared on your Project Dashboard. Start by fixing a date for site inspection.

Keep Updating

Post & check regular updates about progress like design, payments etc. Also you can write feedback, review or complaints on the dashboard at any time.

Completion

After the completion, please mark the project as completed and leave a rating.
